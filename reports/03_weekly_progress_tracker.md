| Risk | Possible Effect | Mitigation |

| Overly technical results | Poor business readability | Translate results into business terms |

## Mitigation

- Maintain a clear weekly plan.
- Focus on business relevance as well as technical quality.
- Keep the number of segments manageable and interpretable.
- Review deliverables regularly to avoid last-minute issues.

## Retrospective

The project is progressing in a structured and manageable way. The most important lesson so far is that business understanding must remain central throughout the analysis. Technical work should always be connected to a clear business purpose. This ensures that the final outputs are not only analytical, but also useful to decision-makers.

## Next Sprint

The next sprint will focus on completing the segmentation analysis, validating the final customer segments, and preparing the full documentation package for submission. The emphasis will be on clarity, professional presentation, and practical business interpretation.
